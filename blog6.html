In recent times, the Construction industry has been grappling with a steep hike in the prices of raw materials like cement, steel, and other raw materials as an outcome of the sudden externals factors .

As a leading-edged Construction firm, it came as a real challenge to us, and we were left with no other choice but to overcome it with an ideal solution.

1) We researched, brainstormed, and came out with innovative materials that are well capable of substituting the major raw materials.
- For instance, we replaced cement with silica fumes and fly ash as they are much cheaper and increase the concrete's strength by 25%.
- Similarly, we have bamboos that can replace steel in providing the structural framework. Implementing these materials will not only help us cut down expenses on raw materials but also aid in sustainable construction.

Since we are in the adaptation mode, we can't deny our technological advancements. #construction
We have specifically devised an application that eases the flow of information between our clients and us. This app makes everything possible with just a click, accessing documents, making payments, and tracking construction progress. In fact, there's more. We have also incorporated technologies like BIM, VR, and the application of drones within the organization to help our architects and designers craft their ideas, showcase the best to our clients, and provide a better understanding through visual representation coupled with data.

We understand the significance of innovation and technology and hence, we are in the middle of adapting them to grow and sustain for our well-being.
