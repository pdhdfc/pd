About Me

As the Chief Executive Officer at Ecoyan Electric Motor Vehicles Pvt Ltd & All In one Hospitality Group, I have led the company through significant growth and transformation. Based in Bangalore Urban, Karnataka, I have been responsible for overseeing all operational aspects, driving strategic initiatives, and fostering a culture of innovation and excellence. My tenure has been marked by a commitment to achieving sustainable business growth and delivering exceptional value to our clients and stakeholders.

Before my current role, I served as Vice President at Housejoy Technologies from May 2020 to January 2021. During this period, I spearheaded sales and business development efforts, focusing on the Interiors, Renovation, and Construction verticals. My strategic approach and leadership skills were instrumental in driving the company's top-line growth and expanding its market presence. Prior to this, I was the AVP | Head of Sales & Business Development at Housejoy, where I led initiatives that significantly boosted the company's revenue and market share.

Recognitions

Throughout my career, I have been honored with several recognitions that underscore my commitment to excellence and leadership in the industry. As CEO of All in One Group, I was awarded the "Business Leader of the Year" in 2022, recognizing my strategic vision and successful implementation of growth initiatives. This accolade was a testament to the innovative approaches and unwavering dedication that I brought to the role, driving significant advancements in the company's operational efficiency and market presence.

In my tenure at Housejoy Technologies, I received the "Excellence in Sales Leadership" award in 2020. This recognition highlighted my ability to lead high-performing teams and achieve remarkable sales growth in the highly competitive interiors, renovation, and construction sectors. Additionally, my earlier contributions at SAMVRIDHI COLONIZERS PRIVATE LIMITED earned me the "Top Sales Director" award for three consecutive years, acknowledging my exceptional performance in market expansion, customer satisfaction, and team management. These accolades reflect my sustained commitment to driving success and my ability to inspire and lead teams towards achieving outstanding results.
